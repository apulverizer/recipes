title:  "Chicken Parm"
tags: 
- cooking
category: dinner

ingredients:
- Boneless chicken breasts
- Bread crumbs
- Spaghetti sauce
- Mozzarella cheese

directions:
- Heat oven to 350˚. 
- Coat chicken in bread crumbs and bake in greased 9x13” pan. 
- Pour spaghetti sauce over the top and add cheese. 
- Bake again until cheese is melted.

---

Serve with spaghetti
