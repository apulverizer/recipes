title:  "Poppy Seed Dressing"
tags: 
- cooking
- vegetarian
category: dressing

ingredients:
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 medium onion
- 1 tsp dry mustard
- 3/4 cup vinegar
- 2 tsp poppy seeds
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup oil

directions:
- Put everything into a blender, except the last two ingredients. 
- Blend well, add sugar, blend well. 
- Add oil, blend until mixture turns white.
