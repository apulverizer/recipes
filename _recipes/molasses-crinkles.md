title:  "Molasses Crinkles"
tags: 
- baking
- vegetarian
category: dessert

ingredients:
- 3/4 cup shortening
- 1 cup brown sugar, packed
- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup molasses
- 2 1/4 cup flour
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp cloves
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp ginger

directions:
- Heat oven to 375˚. 
- Mix wet ingredients. 
- Combine dry ingredients and add to wet ingredients. 
- Chill dough for a few hours. 
- Roll into 1” balls and roll in sugar; place on greased baking sheet. 
- Bake for 10-12 minutes.
