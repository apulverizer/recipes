title:  "Deviled Eggs"
tags:

category: appetizer

ingredients:
- 12 eggs, hard boiled
- Mayo
- Salt and pepper

directions:
- Peel eggs and cut in half. Remove yolks. 
- Mix yolks with mayo, salt, and pepper to taste. 
- Fill egg halves.

Simple deviled eggs, add other ingredients if desired.
