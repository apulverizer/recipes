title:  "Sweet Roll Dough"
tags: 
- baking
- bread
category: topping

ingredients:
- 1 cup warm milk
- 2 eggs
- 1/3 cup margarine, melted
- 4 1/2 cups flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- 2 1/2 tsp Yeast

directions:
- Place dough ingredients into bowl and mix thoroughly. 
- Let the dough rise until it has doubled in size.

---

Sweet dough base for cinnamon rolls.
