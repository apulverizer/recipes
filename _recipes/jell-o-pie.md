- 1-1/16oz sugar-free gelatin (any-flavor)

- 1-8oz container fat-free cool whip

directions:
- Dissolve gelatin in boiling water. 
- Stir in yogurt and fold in whipped topping. 
- Spread into crust. 
- Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
