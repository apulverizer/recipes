title:  "Pumpkin Bread"
tags: 
- baking
- vegetarian
category: dessert

ingredients:
- 3 1/3 cups flour
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 1 ½ tsp salt
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp nutmeg
- 3 cups sugar
- 1 cup oil
- 4 eggs
- 2/3 cup water
- 2 cups canned pumpkin

directions:
- Heat oven to 350˚. 
- Combine dry ingredients, including sugar, in mixing bowl. 
- Make a well in the dry ingredients and add remaining ingredients. 
- Mix until smooth. 
- Grease and flour 3 loaf pans and pour batter into pans. 
- Bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour and then cool.
