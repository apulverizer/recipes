title:  "Death by Chocolate Mousse"

ingredients:
- 21 Oreos, crushed
- 1/4 cup butter, softened
- 3 cup heavy cream, divided
- 12oz chocolate chips
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/8 tsp salt
- 1/4 cup white sugar

directions:
- Heat oven to 350˚ and grease a 9” spring form pan. In a mixing bowl, combine the Oreos and softened butter; then press this mixture into the greased pan. 
- Bake for 5 minutes and then allow it to cool. In a double boiler combine 1 cup heavy cream, chocolate chips, vanilla, and salt and heat until the chocolate is melted and the mixture is smooth. 
- Pour chocolate mixture into a bowl and cool to room temperature, stirring occasionally. 
- In a large bowl beat 2 cups chilled heavy cream with ¼ cup sugar until stiff peaks are formed. 
- Fold whipped cream into chocolate mixture and pour into cooled crust. 
- Chill pie at least 6 hours before serving.
