ingredients:

- 4 cups flour
- 2 tsp active dry yeast
- 1 3/8 cup water
- 3 Tbsp olive or vegetable oil
- (optional) cloves of minced garlic

directions:
- Place all ingredients into a bread machine 
- Run bread machine with dough setting
